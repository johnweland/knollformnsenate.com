# Knoll Wins GOP Endorsement in Senate District 21

Chris Knoll has earned the Republican endorsement in Minnesota Senate District 21, an important milestone for a campaign built on accountability, public safety, fiscal responsibility, and strong support for local families, farmers, and small businesses.

The endorsement reflects growing confidence in Chris's experience, leadership, and commitment to representing the people of District 21 with integrity. As a healthcare executive and licensed provider who works within heavily regulated systems every day, Chris understands how state government decisions affect real people, real businesses, and real communities.

> I am honored to receive the Republican endorsement for Senate District 21 and grateful for the trust and support of grassroots Republicans across the district.

## A Campaign Focused on District 21

This campaign has always been centered on the concerns voters raise at their kitchen tables, in their workplaces, and at community events across the district. Families want safer communities. Taxpayers want responsible budgeting. Parents want schools focused on student success. Employers, farmers, and local entrepreneurs want a state government that respects their work instead of standing in the way.

Chris is running to bring practical, principled leadership to St. Paul and to ensure District 21 has a strong voice fighting for common-sense solutions.

## What Chris Knoll Will Fight For

- Public safety rooted in law, order, and accountability
- Fiscal discipline that respects taxpayers and controls spending
- Support for farmers, producers, and small business owners
- Education that prioritizes core academics and parental involvement
- Government transparency and stronger oversight of taxpayer-funded programs
- Protection of constitutional rights and Minnesota values

## Building Momentum for the General Election

Winning the endorsement is a major step, but it is only the beginning. The campaign is now focused on reaching more voters, growing grassroots support, and sharing Chris's message throughout District 21.

With the endorsement secured, the campaign will continue working to unify supporters around a positive vision for the future: stronger communities, accountable government, and a better path forward for western Minnesota.
