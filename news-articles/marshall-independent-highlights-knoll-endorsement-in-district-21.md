# Marshall Independent Highlights Knoll Endorsement in District 21

The Marshall Independent recently covered the Senate District 21 Republican convention, where Chris Knoll received the party endorsement for the Minnesota Senate seat currently held by Sen. Bill Weber.

The article noted that the endorsement marks an important step for the campaign as attention turns from the convention process to the general election. It also highlighted Chris's message of uniting Republicans across the district and carrying that message directly to voters this fall.

> Chris is ready to unite the party and take a strong message to voters across District 21.

## Recognition From Local News

Coverage from local media matters because it reflects the conversations happening in our communities. The Marshall Independent's report emphasized that this race is about making sure southern Minnesota has a strong voice in St. Paul and that the issues facing rural communities are represented clearly at the Capitol.

For voters across District 21, those priorities include economic opportunity, responsible government, strong communities, and practical leadership rooted in real-world experience.

## Focused on the General Election

As reported in the article, the campaign is now shifting its focus to the general election. That next phase is about reaching more voters, building support across the district, and continuing to talk about the issues that matter most to families, workers, farmers, and business owners.

Chris Knoll is running to offer principled, commonsense leadership and to ensure District 21 is represented by someone who understands both the challenges and the opportunities facing greater Minnesota.

## District 21 Matters

The Marshall Independent article also underscored the size and importance of Senate District 21, which includes communities across southwestern Minnesota. That makes grassroots outreach, local engagement, and a clear message especially important in the months ahead.

The campaign is grateful for the growing support it is receiving and looks forward to sharing Chris's vision with voters throughout the district.
